Meaning from company

Nobody ever tells this model that cat and dog are alike. It is trained on one boring task — predict the next token — in a toy grammar. Yet because same-kind words appear in the same company, they end up needing the same predictions, and the embedding is forced to place them together. Press Train and watch meaning fall out of pure statistics.
